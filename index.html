How to Earn

Players get rewarded by playing and progression in the game. Whenever a new rank is achieved titan will get a reward which he can use to buy in-game items or TITAN-tokens.

In-Game items can be used to upgrade your Titans or to give them special abilities.On adition to than you can purchase skins for any titan you own.
